# Counterpoint as a Condition of Honest Collective Repair

> Counterpoint keeps repair from becoming self-flattery.

A system cannot repair itself if it cannot host a genuinely external voice. Counterpoint is not noise but the difference that allows shared reality to reassemble.

## Apprentice

Repair needs at least one voice that the home story did not already contain.

## Adept

Counterpoint supplies the friction required for honest collective revision. Without an external or at least genuinely differentiated voice, confession curdles into performance and listening becomes self-echo.

## Magus

A living polis requires alterity at the level of epistemic metabolism; otherwise revision collapses into ornamental self-reference.
