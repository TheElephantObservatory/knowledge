Daniel Schmachtenberger is a social philosopher and civilizational researcher who coined the framing of the "metacrisis" — the convergence of interconnected existential risks including AI, ecological breakdown, and geopolitical instability. Co-founder of the Consilience Project and the Civilization Research Institute, his work focuses on identifying the structural drivers of systemic collapse and developing frameworks for collective sensemaking, epistemic integrity, and civilizational design.
