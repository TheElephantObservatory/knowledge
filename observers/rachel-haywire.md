Rachel Haywire (Rachel Marone) is an American artist, writer, and cultural provocateur working at the edge of the avant-garde. Founder of the Extreme Futurist Fest and machineKUNT Records, her work spans experimental industrial music, philosophical essays, and cultural criticism. Her books — including The New Reaction (2015) and Acidexia — apply a Nietzschean lens to transhumanism, cultural decay, and the politics of sovereignty.
