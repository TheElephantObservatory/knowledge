Adam B. Levine has spent over a decade pioneering disruptive technologies before they become mainstream, launching one of the earliest Bitcoin podcasts, Let's Talk Bitcoin! (2013), and founding Tokenly (2014) — one of the earliest companies exploring what could be done with blockchain tokens. He served as CoinDesk's first podcast editor in 2019, hosting shows including Speaking of Bitcoin and Markets Daily, before founding 330.ai in 2021 to build cutting-edge tools for AI-assisted creativity.
