English computer scientist and theoretical physicist, creator of CFEngine (1993) — the first configuration management system — and originator of Promise Theory, a formal model of voluntary cooperation in distributed systems. His book Thinking in Promises applies these ideas to software infrastructure, organizational design, and knowledge systems. A former professor at Oslo Metropolitan University, he now works as an independent researcher and technology advisor at the intersection of physics, computation, and complexity.
