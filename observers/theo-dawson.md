Award-winning developmental psychologist and founder of Lectica, a nonprofit that develops evidence-based assessments for measuring cognitive complexity across the lifespan. She created the Lectical Assessment System to diagnose how people reason rather than just what they know, with funding from the NIH and US Department of Education. Her research challenges high-stakes testing culture by building tools that support ongoing developmental growth.
