Germane Marvel is an independent philosopher and writer whose work applies metamodernism, Black philosophy, and integral theory to questions of emergence, consciousness, and cultural transformation. Writing primarily on Medium, their work explores Black metamodernism, nested emergence theory, decolonizing AI, and metatheism — building philosophical frameworks that bridge integral consciousness with African-American intellectual traditions.
