American philosopher and former rabbi who developed the concept of Unique Self — an integral theory of personhood synthesizing Kabbalistic mysticism, Ken Wilber's integral framework, and evolutionary spirituality. Co-founder with Wilber of the Center for Integral Wisdom and author of Your Unique Self, his work proposes a path beyond both traditional religion and secular individualism.
