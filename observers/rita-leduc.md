Interdisciplinary artist and researcher whose practice operates at the intersection of art, ecology, and systems thinking. Her work investigates pathways of potential within living systems through fieldwork, sensorial inquiry, and visual translation — explored through projects such as Extending Ecology (a collaboration at Hubbard Brook Experimental Forest) and the Oika living philosophy. She teaches in Rutgers University's interdisciplinary Creative Expression and the Environment program.
