Brent Cooper is a philosopher, writer, and executive director of The Abs-Tract Organization (TATO), a meta think tank based in Vancouver dedicated to absolute social philosophy and global civil society. His work conceptualizes “abstraction” as a process of complexity reduction for solving systemic social problems, situated within a broader metamodernist project for post-ideological, globally responsive civilization. He writes extensively on Medium and has been involved in metacrisis and civilizational risk research networks.
