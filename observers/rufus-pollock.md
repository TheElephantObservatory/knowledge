British economist and activist who founded the Open Knowledge Foundation in 2004, catalyzing the global open data movement with tools like CKAN and the Open Definition. His book The Open Revolution (2018) argues that in the digital age, whoever controls information controls the future, making the case for knowledge as a shared commons over private monopoly. He also co-founded Life Itself, a community exploring collective wisdom and cultural transformation.
