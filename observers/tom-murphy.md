Tom Murphy is Professor Emeritus of Physics at UC San Diego who spent his academic career leading a lunar laser ranging project testing General Relativity before redirecting his focus to energy, ecology, and civilizational limits. His influential “Do the Math” blog applies physics and estimation to assess energy systems, economic growth, and sustainable futures, evolving from quantitative energy analysis toward a deeper philosophical critique of modernity and ecological health.
