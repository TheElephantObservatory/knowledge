Tristan Harris is a technology ethicist and co-founder of the Center for Humane Technology who served as a design ethicist at Google before leaving to build a nonprofit dedicated to addressing the systemic harms of the attention economy. He became a household name through his central role in the Netflix documentary The Social Dilemma and the podcast Your Undivided Attention, and has since expanded his focus to the societal risks of artificial intelligence.
