Philosopher, Socratic counselor, and co-founder of Socratic Counseling, whose work centers on the meaning crisis in contemporary Western culture. He co-authored Zombies in Western Culture: A Twenty-First Century Crisis (2017) and Awakening from the Meaning Crisis (2024) with cognitive scientist John Vervaeke, drawing on Plato, Kierkegaard, existential theology, and depth psychology. His practice translates philosophical dialogue into a method for helping people recover coherence, purpose, and self-understanding.
