Stanford-educated entrepreneur and writer best known for coining the term "memetic warfare" in a 2015 paper for NATO's Strategic Communications Journal, arguing that nations must develop capabilities to contest influence operations on social media. He later founded the Boyd Institute, a nonprofit think tank focused on space, geopolitics, and technology governance. His work ranges from information theory and national security to consciousness and the deeper forces shaping civilization.
