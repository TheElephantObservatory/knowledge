Applied complexity scientist affiliated with the New England Complex Systems Institute, whose research focuses on systemic risk, precaution, and the design of robust policies for large-scale systems. He draws on principles like subsidiarity and the precautionary principle, advocating decentralization and localism as strategies for managing complexity in hyperconnected societies.
