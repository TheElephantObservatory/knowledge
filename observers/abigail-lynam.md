Abigail Lynam is a coach, facilitator, and faculty member at Pacific Integral whose work draws on hermeneutic phenomenology, relational ontology, and contemplative practice to support transformation in individuals and collectives. She contributes to integral and developmental coaching through a framework she calls the “seven facets of awakened wholeness,” integrating cognitive, somatic, relational, and spiritual dimensions of human development. Her research explores the interpenetration of individual and collective transformation as a foundation for emergence and collective intelligence.
