Daniel L. Garner is a philosopher and independent researcher whose work engages the meaning crisis, relational ontology, and the cultural conditions required for civilizational renewal. He is the author of The Map Is Indestructible and hosts philosophical conversations at Philosophy Portal, drawing on hermeneutics, sensemaking, and media ecology to examine the relationship between culture, consciousness, and social transformation.
