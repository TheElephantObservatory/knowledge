Steve March is an independent practitioner-scholar and founder of Aletheia, a coach training school in the San Francisco Bay Area dedicated to what he calls “next-generation coaching.” His intellectual work draws on Continental philosophy, hermeneutic phenomenology, depth psychology, and nondual spiritual traditions to articulate a vision of psychospiritual development that integrates cognitive, somatic, relational, and spiritual dimensions. He has been a professional coach for over 22 years and taught at New Ventures West for more than a decade.
