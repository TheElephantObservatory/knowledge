Independent process philosopher and educator whose work draws on process ontology, cognitive development theory, and contemplative practices to reimagine leadership and organizational design. She originated the Open Participatory Organization model and runs The Pop-Up School, a learning community for post-conventional thinkers. Her approach integrates philosophical rigor from figures like Whitehead and Bergson with hands-on organizational and developmental practice.
