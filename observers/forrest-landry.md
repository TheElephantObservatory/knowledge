American philosopher, engineer, and independent researcher whose work spans applied metaphysics, systems design, ethics, and existential risk. His major work, An Immanent Metaphysics (2023), presents a foundational framework for understanding being, choice, and the conditions for a good life — developed alongside his founding of Magic-Flight and consulting work for government agencies. He is a Research Scholar at the Ronin Institute and regards his philosophical and design work as inseparable.
