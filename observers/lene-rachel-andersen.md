Danish author, economist, futurist, and philosopher best known for co-authoring The Nordic Secret, which traces Scandinavian societal success to a century-long project of cultivating psychological complexity through Bildung. Her book Metamodernity proposes a new cultural code weaving together indigenous, pre-modern, modern, and postmodern wisdom traditions to meet the challenges of technological acceleration and global disruption. She is President of Nordic Bildung, co-founder of the Global Bildung Network, and a full member of the Club of Rome.
