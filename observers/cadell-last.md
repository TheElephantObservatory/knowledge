Cadell Last is a philosopher, evolutionary anthropologist, and independent researcher whose work bridges Hegelian dialectics, psychoanalytic theory, and the deep evolutionary history of human consciousness. He is the author of 'Global Brain Singularity' and 'Sex, Masculinity, God' and hosts the podcast 'The Dialectical Futurist.' Last engages questions of post-capitalism, technological acceleration, and the spiritual dimensions of human becoming.
