Cognitive scientist working at the intersection of rationality, relevance realization, and living-systems ethics. Collaborating with philosopher John Vervaeke, she developed the framework of Autopoiethics — an ethics grounded in self-organizing systems and lived integrity rather than computational utility calculations — as a critique of reductive effective altruism. Her research challenges the computational paradigm in cognitive science and advocates for cognitive sovereignty as a foundation for healthy collective intelligence.
