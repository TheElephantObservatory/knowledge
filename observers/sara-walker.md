Theoretical physicist and astrobiologist at Arizona State University whose work focuses on the origin of life and what distinguishes living from non-living matter. She is a co-developer of assembly theory, a new scientific framework for measuring the molecular complexity and causal history embedded in objects, with direct applications to detecting life on other planets. She serves as Deputy Director of the Beyond Center for Fundamental Concepts in Science and is External Faculty at the Santa Fe Institute.
