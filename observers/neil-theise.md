Professor of pathology at NYU Grossman School of Medicine and pioneer in adult stem cell plasticity and the anatomy of the human interstitium. His 2023 book Notes on Complexity applies complexity science to medicine, consciousness, and the nature of being, arguing that the universe itself is one vast self-organizing system. He collaborates across integrative medicine, consciousness studies, and science-religion dialogue.
