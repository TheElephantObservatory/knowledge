Welsh knowledge management researcher and consultant who created the Cynefin framework, a widely-used sense-making model that distinguishes clear, complicated, complex, and chaotic decision environments. Founder of Cognitive Edge (now The Cynefin Company), he spent years at IBM developing narrative-based knowledge management approaches before applying complexity science to organizational strategy and public policy. His work synthesizes systems thinking, anthropology, and cognitive science.
