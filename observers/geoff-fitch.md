Geoff Fitch is a co-founder and senior faculty member at Pacific Integral, a developmental coaching and transformative education organization. With over 25 years of experience drawing on somatic psychology, transpersonal psychology, integral theory, and collective intelligence, he developed the internationally-acclaimed Generating Transformative Change (GTC) program, now in its 24th cohort and offered on three continents. His work centers on cultivating embodied presence, relational depth, and developmental growth in individuals and organizations.
