Bernardo Kastrup is a Dutch philosopher and computer engineer who serves as Executive Director of Essentia Foundation, a nonprofit dedicated to consciousness studies and metaphysics. Holding PhDs in both computer engineering (Eindhoven University of Technology) and philosophy (Radboud University), his work advances analytic idealism — the view that consciousness is the primary substrate of reality and individual minds are dissociated segments of a universal field of experience. He has published 11 books and his ideas have appeared in Scientific American, the American Philosophical Association, and Big Think.
