Former CEO of Network Solutions who negotiated its $15 billion acquisition by Verisign, Jim Rutt is now a Distinguished Fellow and Trustee Emeritus at the Santa Fe Institute, where he chaired the board from 2009–2012. Through The Jim Rutt Show podcast and his leadership in the Game B movement, he explores complexity science, social evolution, and what it would take to replace the current "Game A" civilizational operating system with something more adaptive and humane.
