Marianne Williamson is an American author, spiritual teacher, and political activist best known for her interpretation of A Course in Miracles in her seminal book A Return to Love. She has authored over a dozen books on spirituality, politics, and healing, and ran for U.S. President in both 2020 and 2024 as a Democratic candidate advocating for a politics of love, structural reform, and moral imagination. She hosts TRANSFORM with Marianne Williamson on Substack and remains a leading voice at the intersection of spiritual and political life.
