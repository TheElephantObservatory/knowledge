Steve McIntosh is an American integral philosopher, author, and co-founder of the Institute for Cultural Evolution, a think tank in Boulder, Colorado dedicated to addressing political polarization through a developmental lens. Drawing on Pierre Teilhard de Chardin and Alfred North Whitehead, his work argues that culture evolves through distinct stages — traditional, modern, postmodern — and that transcending polarization requires integrating the values of each. He has authored five books including Integral Consciousness, Developmental Politics, and the forthcoming Truth Emerging (2026).
