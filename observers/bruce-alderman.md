Bruce Alderman is an integral and transpersonal psychologist, poet, and associate director of the Blue Sky Leaders program at the California Institute of Integral Studies. Affiliate faculty at JFK University and National University, his work spans metatheory, integral philosophy, and contemplative practice. He co-hosts The Integral Stage podcast with Layman Pascal, exploring integral, metamodern, and holistic approaches to complex social, ecological, and spiritual challenges.
