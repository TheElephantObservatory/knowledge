Associate Professor in the Philosophy, Cosmology, and Consciousness program at the California Institute of Integral Studies, Segall is a transdisciplinary philosopher whose work bridges Alfred North Whitehead's process-relational metaphysics with German Idealism and philosophy of nature. His books — including Physics of the World-Soul (2021) and Crossing the Threshold (2023) — argue for a re-enchanted cosmology that integrates the natural sciences with questions of mind, life, and meaning.
