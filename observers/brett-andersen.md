Independent writer and researcher whose Substack publication Intimations of a New Worldview draws on evolutionary biology, developmental psychology, complexity science, and cognitive science to articulate an emerging post-postmodern worldview. His central thesis connects biological and cognitive complexification, arguing that humanity's capacity to discover and enact non-zero-sum cooperation represents optimal psychological and civilizational functioning. He is working on a book synthesizing hundreds of texts across these fields.
