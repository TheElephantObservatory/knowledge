Lisa Norton is an educator, designer, and integral scholar affiliated with the Institut für Integrale Studien (IFIS) in Freiburg, Germany. Former Professor of Design Leadership at Parsons School of Design and Professor at the School of the Art Institute of Chicago, her work explores the intersection of design, systems change, and psychological transformation. She hosts Uncertainment Lounge, a social-emotional learning program, and is developing Design Being, a psychological pattern language for designers.
