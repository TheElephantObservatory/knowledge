Rob Scott is the creator of Identity Shifting, a coaching methodology for transforming unconscious limits and core beliefs that he developed after navigating personal challenges including abuse, addiction, and cancer. His practice helps high achievers identify and transform the deep-seated psychological patterns holding them back, drawing on principles of subconscious reprogramming and identity-level change. He hosts the Fundamental Shift and Rob Scott Show podcasts and runs the Fundamental Shift coaching program.
