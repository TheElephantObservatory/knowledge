Susanne Cook-Greuter is an independent scholar and one of the foremost authorities on adult ego development theory, having spent over 45 years extending Jane Loevinger’s sentence completion method. She holds an EdD from Harvard University and developed the Maturity Assessment Profile (MAP), a validated instrument for measuring vertical development across nine stages of meaning-making. Her work has become foundational to coaching, leadership development, and integral psychology worldwide.
