Daniel Görtz is a political philosopher and sociologist who co-founded Metamoderna, a think tank publishing metamodern books and ideas from Sweden. Co-author (writing as “Hanzi Freinacht”) of The Listening Society and Nordic Ideology, he leads what he calls “the Nordic school” of metamodernism and works as In-House Philosopher at Glimworks. His work applies integral theory in secular and practical directions toward integral politics, culture, and social transformation.
