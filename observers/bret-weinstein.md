Evolutionary biologist who spent 14 years as a professor at Evergreen State College before resigning in 2017 following campus protests, Weinstein co-hosts the DarkHorse Podcast with his wife and fellow biologist Heather Heying. Together they co-authored A Hunter-Gatherer's Guide to the 21st Century, applying evolutionary theory to modern culture, medicine, and what they see as systemic failures in institutions shaped by short-term incentives.
