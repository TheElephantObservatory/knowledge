Mmabatho Montse is a South African entrepreneur, writer, and podcast host based in Pretoria who works at the intersection of human development, social entrepreneurship, and women’s empowerment. She holds a Master’s from Wits School of Governance on women’s social entrepreneurship and hosts Conscious Conversations with Mmabatho Montse, exploring healing, resilience, and human expansion. Her work bridges integral and relational frameworks with the lived realities of community transformation in Africa.
